# Cinder Research — Guidelines

## Stance
Swiss / data-dense. Strict 12-col grid, hairline rules, generous numerical labelling, one hot accent.
Dark canvas (near-black ground, paper-white text). Monospace-as-display.

## Type
- Display & labels: JetBrains Mono (uppercase, tight tracking for labels; normal for headlines)
- Body & UI text: Inter Tight
- Editorial flourish (abstract pulls): Newsreader italic, used sparingly

## Color
- Ground: #0B0B0C
- Paper: #F4F4EE
- Hairline: rgba(244,244,238,0.12)
- Accent: #D9FF3F (electric chartreuse) — links, focus, single CTA, status dots
- Muted text: #8A8A82

## Layout
- 12-col grid, gutters 24px, page margin 32–48px
- Section labels: "§ 01 / RESEARCH" in mono uppercase, tracked
- Numeric metadata everywhere — dates, paper IDs, percent, ranges
- Hairline horizontal rules between sections, never heavy borders

## Voice
Precise, understated, technical. No marketing language. Real-sounding research titles, dates 2024-2026.
